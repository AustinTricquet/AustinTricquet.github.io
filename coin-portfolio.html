Coin Portfolio - Work in progress

Track performance and manage crypto assets in a non-custodial wallet aggregator application. Built with React, Redux, 3box, Firebase and Ethereum smart contracts. Using web3.js to communicate and execute smart contracts on Ethereum blockchain to read and update data for the portfolio feature. Using Axios and APIs to provide a live search suggestion feature for researching and tracking crypto assets.

Main Features

  • Portfolio - See all your wallets in one place, Bitcoin, Ethereum, and more to come!
  • Markets - Track and research crypto markets with your own watchlist!
  • Trades - See all your trades and filter by wallet or asset!
  • Taxes - Take the tax headache away and get a tax report for all your trading!
