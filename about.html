During a project involving Python in my role as an Industrial Engineer, I quickly discovered that the more I learned about programming the more I fell in love with it. Since then, my career path has become clear - to master web development and launch myself into a career as a Web Developer.

Armed with a strong engineering background, I bring a refined analytical approach to building solutions that solve real problems. Though my journey won’t be easy, I’m determined to succeed.

Along this path I’ve already built a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Python and the basics of SQL. I’ve also been trained in Agile SDLC and have a green belt in Lean Six Sigma. Continuing my education online, I’m currently learning front end and back end technologies including Angular, jQuery, Node JS, Express, MongoDB and RESTful APIs.

I love discovering the perfect solution for the problem in my code and see all my hard work come together to make a website or other software work as designed! I love the strategy and logic behind everything I do with code. I embrace the struggle, learn from the failures and leverage them to grow both inside and outside of the office.

When I’m not working, I’m either trying a new restaurant with my wife, Courtney, or going on a day trip to a place we’ve never been before. We love to travel, and hope to go abroad soon! If we are stuck at home, we aim to watch our favorite shows on Netflix (mostly just the Office) or we end up nerding out with Star Wars. Last but not least, we both enjoy playing with our Golden Retriever puppy, Bailey.
