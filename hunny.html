Meet Hunny

Plan More, Worry Less!

Hunny is designed to help you get a better understanding of your personal finances. Most financial apps focus more on what you spent. However with Hunny, we take a different approach and make the focus more on your budget planning. No painful tracking of each purchase, no linking of credit cards, just your money made sweet. With a clear view of your money, you can stay on top of every bill and know your financial situation better!

Main Features

  • Budget - See where every dollar you earn is put to work!
  • Calendar - See all your upcoming bills and paychecks!
  • Goals - Set saving goals from a big vacation, to that new video game!
  • Accounts - Mirror your Bank accounts to help put your plan into action!

Tech Stack
